Features - HDMI connectivity, Bluetooth support, built-in equalizer, night mode.#

From HDMI connectivity that ensures seamless integration with your TV and gaming consoles to Bluetooth support for wireless streaming of music and podcasts, the Polk Magnifi Mini has got you covered. Plus, the built-in equalizer lets you fine-tune the sound to your liking, while the night mode is perfect for late-night listening without disturbing the neighbors.

But these features are just the tip of the iceberg! Here’s a deeper dive into what makes the Polk Magnifi Mini so special:

  • HDMI ARC (Audio Return Channel): This feature allows you to connect the sound bar to your TV with a single HDMI cable, eliminating the need for separate audio cables.

  • Bluetooth 5.0: The latest Bluetooth technology provides a stable and reliable wireless connection for streaming music and podcasts from your smartphone, tablet, or laptop.

  • Built-in Equalizer: With six preset EQ modes, you can easily adjust the sound to match the content you’re watching or listening to.

  • Night Mode: This mode reduces the bass and boosts the dialogue, making it easier to hear what’s being said in movies and TV shows without waking up the whole house.

  • HDMI ARC: Single cable connection for both audio and video.
  • Bluetooth 5.0: Stable and reliable wireless music streaming.
  • Built-in Equalizer: Six preset EQ modes for customizable sound.
  • Night Mode: Reduced bass and boosted dialogue for late-night listening.
